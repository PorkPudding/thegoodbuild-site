Return & Warranty Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At The Good Build, we stand behind every system that leaves our bench.

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 calendar days from pickup to return your PC for a full refund.

  • System must be in the same condition received — no physical damage or unauthorized modifications.
  • All original accessories, cables, and packaging must be included.
  • Contact us within 30 days to initiate.

Defective Hardware

If something turns out to be defective, contact us immediately. We'll repair, replace, or fully refund at your choice.

Ongoing Tech Support

After the warranty window, we don't disappear. Free ongoing technical support for every system — driver issues, component troubleshooting, upgrade advice. We're here to help.

Manufacturer Warranties

Many components carry their own manufacturer warranty. We'll help you identify coverage and navigate claims if needed.

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage after delivery (drops, spills, power surges)
  • Software issues unrelated to our installation
  • Modifications by someone other than The Good Build
  • Normal wear and tear

For returns, warranty, or support: email support@thegoodbuild.shop
