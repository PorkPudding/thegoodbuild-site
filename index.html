About
One builder. No BS.

The Good Build started in a garage in Corona, California. What began as a hobby — buying, building, and flipping PCs — turned into something bigger when people kept coming back and bringing their friends.

Every system you see on this site was built by me. I source the parts, I assemble the machine, I test it, and I stand behind it. There's no warehouse, no investors, no corporate nonsense. Just good builds at fair prices from someone who genuinely loves doing this.

All components are thoroughly cleaned, tested, and stress-tested before any build is listed. I treat every system like I'm building it for myself.

FAQ
Common questions
If yours isn't here, reach out — we respond fast.

How do I buy one of your PCs?

▼
Message me through the contact page or DM @romans_goodbuild on Instagram. We'll set up a local meetup in Corona, CA — you see the PC running, then pay in person. No online checkout, no shipping roulette.

What's your return policy?

▼
We offer a 30-day return window from the date of pickup. If something's wrong, reach out within 30 days and we'll make it right — full refund. See our full Return & Warranty Policy.

Where are you located? Do you ship?

▼
Corona, California. Sales are local — Corona, Riverside, the Inland Empire, and Orange County. Buying in person means you see the machine running before you pay. Not local? Reach out anyway; if it's a good fit we can talk options.

Are these new or used parts?

▼
Our builds use refurbished components — previously used parts that have been fully disassembled, cleaned, tested, and stress-tested. Each listing clearly states component details. We only use parts that meet our quality standards.

What comes installed?

▼
Every build ships with a clean Windows 11 Pro installation with the latest drivers and updates. No bloatware, no trial software. Just a fresh OS ready to go.

Can I request a custom build?

▼
Not at this time — we sell pre-built, ready-to-ship systems. If you have something specific in mind, reach out and we'll let you know if we can make it happen.

Do you offer a warranty?

▼
90-day hardware warranty on every build covering parts and labor. Plus free ongoing tech support. Individual components may carry their own manufacturer warranty — we'll help you navigate those claims if needed.

Return & Warranty Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At The Good Build, we stand behind every system that leaves our bench.

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 calendar days from pickup to return your PC for a full refund.

  • System must be in the same condition received — no physical damage or unauthorized modifications.
  • All original accessories, cables, and packaging must be included.
  • Contact us within 30 days to initiate.

Defective Hardware

If something turns out to be defective, contact us immediately. We'll repair, replace, or fully refund at your choice.

Ongoing Tech Support

After the warranty window, we don't disappear. Free ongoing technical support for every system — driver issues, component troubleshooting, upgrade advice. We're here to help.

Manufacturer Warranties

Many components carry their own manufacturer warranty. We'll help you identify coverage and navigate claims if needed.

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage after delivery (drops, spills, power surges)
  • Software issues unrelated to our installation
  • Modifications by someone other than The Good Build
  • Normal wear and tear
