Return & Warranty Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At TheGoodBuild LLC, we stand behind every system that leaves our bench. Here's exactly what you're covered for.

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

  • System must be returned in the same condition received — no physical damage, no missing components.
  • All original packaging must be used for return shipping.
  • Contact us within 30 days to initiate — do not ship anything back without authorization.

Defective or damaged? We cover return shipping both ways — full refund upon inspection.

Change of mind? We still accept the return — buyer covers return shipping, full refund issued upon inspection.

90-Day Hardware Warranty

Every build includes a 90-day hardware warranty covering parts and labor on manufacturer defects and hardware failures under normal use.

  • Coverage begins on the date of delivery.
  • Covers: component failure, DOA issues, intermittent hardware faults (crashes, BSODs, artifacting).
  • If a warranty issue arises, contact us first — most issues can be diagnosed remotely before any shipping is needed.
  • Confirmed warranty repairs ship back to us on our dime.

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage caused by the buyer — drops, liquid spills, bent pins, broken components.
  • Software issues caused by the buyer — viruses, corrupted OS, driver conflicts from third-party installs.
  • Damage from improper use — overclocking, unauthorized component removal, inadequate ventilation.
  • Damage from external factors — power surges, lightning, flooding.
  • Parts failure outside the 90-day warranty window.

Ongoing Tech Support

After the warranty window, we don't disappear. Free ongoing technical support for every system — driver issues, upgrade advice, troubleshooting. You're talking to the person who built your PC.

Manufacturer Warranties

Many components carry their own manufacturer warranty that may extend beyond our 90-day coverage. We'll help you identify and navigate those claims if needed.
